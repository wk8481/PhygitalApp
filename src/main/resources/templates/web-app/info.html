Our Mission

At Levuur and Tree Company, our mission is to empower youth and foster inclusive decision-making processes. We believe that young voices are essential for creating positive change in communities. Our goal is to bridge the gap between young people and decision-makers by providing accessible platforms for meaningful engagement.

Our Approach

We combine digital innovation with participatory methodologies to create inclusive spaces where young people feel heard and valued. By leveraging our expertise in stakeholder management and technology, we design customized solutions that empower youth to contribute their ideas and perspectives to important policy issues.

Our Project

Currently, we're developing a "Phygital" tool to obtain youth input on policy issues. This innovative tool combines physical installations with digital technology to create immersive experiences that engage young people in local governance. Our aim is to empower young people to participate actively in decision-making processes and shape the future of their communities.

Join us in empowering young voices and shaping the future together.
